Justice Bio

Justice Georgie is an artist who is passionate about giving back to his community. Justice is a member of the Youth As Resources Board of Directors, a performing artist with WombWork Productions and a communication specialist with Youth Rising Coalition. Justice is the owner and founder of Embrace My World Unlimited. Embrace My World is a media storytelling social enterprise aimed to address issues that impact people of color and produce and promote videos that encourage youth to think critically and take action.
